ML PIPELINE

How It Works

01
Data Collection & Cleaning
Kaggle MBTI dataset with 8,600+ rows of user posts paired with personality labels. URLs, stop words, and noise removed. Text lowercased and normalized.
02
Feature Engineering
TF-IDF vectorization extracts word-frequency features. Additional features: post count per user, average post length, frequency of type-related keywords.
03
Model Training & Evaluation
Four classifiers — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, and XGBoost — trained and evaluated using accuracy, F1-score, and confusion matrices for all 16 classes.
